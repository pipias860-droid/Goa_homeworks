Death note


Creators: Tsugumi Ohba (Writer) and Takeshi Obata (Artist). Genre: Supernatural / Psychological Thriller / Crime Drama. Core Plot: A high school genius named Light Yagami finds a supernatural notebook that kills anyone whose name is written in it. He decides to use it to "cleanse" the world of criminals and become a god.
