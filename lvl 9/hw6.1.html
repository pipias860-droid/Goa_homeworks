**Chuck Norris** is an American martial artist, actor, and former professional fighter, born in 1940. He became famous for his roles in action movies and the TV series **Walker, Texas Ranger**, as well as for his martial arts expertise. He is also widely known in popular culture for the “Chuck Norris facts” internet memes highlighting his legendary strength and toughness. Profession: Actor, Martial Artist Notable works: *Walker, Texas Ranger*, *Way of the Dragon* Martial arts: Black belt in multiple disciplines
