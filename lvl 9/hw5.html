Interstellar is a science-fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan and released in 2014. The story follows a former NASA pilot, Cooper (played by Matthew McConaughey), who travels through a wormhole in space to find a new habitable planet for humanity because Earth is slowly becoming unlivable. The movie explores themes like time, gravity, space travel, love, and survival. It is known for its realistic science (with help from physicist Kip Thorne), emotional story, and amazing soundtrack by Hans Zimmer. Genre: Sci-fi, Drama Release year: 2014 IMDb rating: 8.6/10
