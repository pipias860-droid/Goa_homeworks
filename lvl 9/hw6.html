**Mike Tyson** is an American former professional boxer, born in 1966. He became the youngest heavyweight champion in history at age 20 and was known for his aggressive style, power, and speed in the ring. Tyson is a former undisputed heavyweight champion and one of the most famous and controversial figures in boxing history, with a career marked by both legendary victories and personal challenges. Profession: Boxer Nickname: “Iron Mike” Active years: 1985 2005
