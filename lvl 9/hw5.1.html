Neon genesis evangelion

**Neon Genesis Evangelion** is a Japanese anime series created by Hideaki Anno, first aired in 1995. The story follows a group of teenagers, mainly Shinji Ikari, who pilot giant mechs called Evangelions to fight mysterious beings known as Angels, while dealing with personal trauma and complex relationships. It’s famous for its psychological depth, existential themes, and influence on anime culture. Genre: Mecha, Sci-Fi, Psychological Episodes: 26 (original series) Known for: Deep psychological storytelling and cultural impact
