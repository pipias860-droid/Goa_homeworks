Compass

A compass is a tool that helps people find directions.

It shows the four main directions: North, South, East, and West.

Main Directions

  • North
  • South
  • East
  • West

Why is a Compass Important?

A compass is important because it helps travelers, hikers, sailors, and explorers know where they are going.

Types of Compass Use

  1. Traveling
  2. Hiking
  3. Camping
  4. Navigation
