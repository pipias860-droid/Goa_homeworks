The Ferrari F50 is a legendary supercar released in 1995 by Ferrari to celebrate the company’s 50th anniversary. It is powered by a 4.7-liter V12 engine producing around 520 horsepower, with technology closely inspired by Formula 1.

The car can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in about 3.7 seconds and has a top speed of around 325 km/h. Only 349 units were ever built, making it extremely rare and valuable among collectors. The F50 is also known for its minimal electronic driving aids, giving it a raw and pure driving experience.
